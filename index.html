    Welcome

    We are the newest varsity CIF sport at Torrey Pines High School, founded in the 2014-2015 school year and joining the roster of spring sports. We are the only North County school with a badminton team, and we compete in the Eastern League with Mira Mesa, Scripps Ranch, La Jolla, Mission Bay, and Cathedral Catholic. Our team fields 5 events: men's singles, men's doubles, women's singles, women's doubles, and mixed doubles.
