    • Welcome

    We are the newest varsity CIF sport at Torrey Pines High School, founded in the 2014-2015 school year and joining the roster of spring sports. We are the only San Dieguito school with a badminton team, and we compete in the Eastern League with Kearny, Madison, Point Loma, Crawford, La Jolla, and Hoover. Our team fields 5 events: men's singles, men's doubles, women's singles, women's doubles, and mixed doubles.

    Tuan Tran

    Assistant Coach

    Tuan Tran (alongside Lars Trupe) is Torrey Pines High School's first badminton coach. From 2012 to present, he has assistant coached badminton at San Diego Miramar College, Cuyamaca College, and Grossmont College. He is recently a gold medalist of the 2016 California State Games Tournament. He is most well known for his knowledge and explanation of the mechanics of badminton.

    Doreen Hom

    Parent Liaison

    dhomkng@aol.com

    Doreen Hom is Torrey Pines Badminton's official team manager. She is in charge of uniforms, team budgets, coordinating rides, and everything necessary to ensure the success of the team. She lettered in badminton at San Diego High School then played at University of California, San Diego.
